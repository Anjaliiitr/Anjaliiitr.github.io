Bio

I have done my M.Tech and Ph.D in the field of Medical Image Analysis from G.B. Pant Engineering College Pauri Garhwal and IIT Roorkee respectively. During Ph.D., I got a young scientist award for my work by Uttarakhand Council for Science & Technology in 2018. Before joining IIIT Allahabad, I have served DIT University Dehradun, University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) Dehradun and Women Institute of Technology Dehradun for some time. Currently, I am working on two sponsored projects funded by IIIT Allahabad and Science and Engineering Research Board (SERB).
